About Me

Hi! I'm Ali Asghari, a Master's student in Economics at FAU University with a passion for econometrics, statistical methods, and data visualization. My research interests focus on inequality, economic mobility, and development economics.

I currently work as a student assistant under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Christine Binzel, and at the Ludwig Erhard ifo Center for Social Market Economy and Institutional Economics.

My work combines econometric analysis with modern web technologies to create meaningful visualizations and analytical tools. I'm particularly interested in data-driven storytelling using distributed information to make complex economic concepts accessible and engaging.
